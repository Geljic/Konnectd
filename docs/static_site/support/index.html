Konnectd Support

Need help with your account, a puzzle, a friend challenge, or a report?

Email us and include your device, app version, account email or handle if relevant, and a short description of what happened.

Email support@konnectd.xyz

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app from your profile. If you cannot access your account, email support from the address used for your account.

Reports and Safety

You can report puzzles from completed puzzle review and report or block users from friend profiles. Email support for urgent safety issues.
