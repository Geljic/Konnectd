Konnectd Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Konnectd is a daily word puzzle game with optional accounts, friends, challenges, ads, and notifications. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

If you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, username tag, password authentication data, game stats, play history, ratings, friends, challenge records, reports, blocks, and notification token if you enable notifications.

If you play as a guest, game progress may be stored locally on your device.

Ads and Third-Party Services

Konnectd may show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and platform services may collect device, advertising, diagnostics, and usage data according to their own policies. We request non-personalized ads where supported.

How We Use Information

We use data to operate accounts, save progress, calculate stats and leaderboards, support friend challenges, send optional notifications, improve puzzle quality, prevent abuse, and provide support.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Some profile and gameplay information, such as display name, handle, leaderboard stats, and challenge results, may be visible to other players where the app feature requires it. Service providers may process data for hosting, notifications, ads, and app operations.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app from your profile. Deleting your account removes account-linked profile data and related gameplay/social records where required for the service to stop identifying you.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact support@konnectd.xyz.
